Osahon Okoro

Osahon Okoro is a Nigerian academic and researcher at the University of Calabar, where he lectures in the Department of Computer Science. He holds a background in Electrical/Electronics Engineering and Computer Science and is pursuing a Ph.D. His work focuses on areas like Internet of Things (IoT) for rural communities, wireless networks, renewable energy solutions, and network security. Beyond teaching and research, he is also described as a poet and a religious person.
